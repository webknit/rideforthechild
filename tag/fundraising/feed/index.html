It will be hosted by Stepney Hill Farm in their impressive barn.

This date is only two days before I fly to America so in addition to raising lots of money for the charities, it’s also an opportunity for my close family and friends to say their goodbyes should I perish whilst trying to ride America.

Invites will be going out closer to the date but almost anyone who wants to support this cause is more than welcome. The itinerary will be confirmed at a later date (there are some good ideas flying about), but a good day/night is guaranteed!

One of the fundraising ideas at this party is a raffle. I am actively contacting businesses in the hope of them donating a prizes towards the raffle. If you could potentially donate something, or know someone else who might be interested, then please shoot me an email on contact@rideforthechild.co.uk.
